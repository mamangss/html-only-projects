Kamikazee
Kamikazee is a Filipino rock band formed in 2000. The band is composed of Jay Contreras, Jomal Linao, Led Tuyay, Puto Astete and Bords Burdeos. Fans and critics have described the band's music as a mix of pop-punk and hard rock.
Record Label Universal Records Philippines
Genres Pinoy rock, Alternative rock, Pop punk, Hard rock, Hard core
